Gru, a bad guy who has changed, gets used to being a dad in Despicable Me 2. The Anti-Villain League asks Gru to help them find the bad guys who stole a powerful mutagen. Along with his adopted daughters and the cute Minions, Gru goes on an exciting adventure that tries his newfound bravery and shows him making allies he didn't expect.
